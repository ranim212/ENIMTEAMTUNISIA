Who Are We?

We are a team of engineering students from ENIM (National engineering school of Monastir), guided by our passionate professors and united by a shared vision of innovation and sustainability.

Founded in 2015, our group was established with the purpose of participating in the Shell Eco-marathon, in which we participated for the first time in France in June 2016.

Our first mission was to design and build a vehicle for the Prototype ‚Äì Electric Vehicle category. Today, we are competing in the Urban Concept ‚Äì Autonomous Electric Vehicle category, where we have developed and showcased a self-driving vehicle that combines high energy efficiency with eco-friendly design.
